      ### Exploratory data analysis
      ---
      I explored the data to answer key questions such as:
      - Total sales made.
      - Daily sales made.
      - Top sales at each time of the day.
      - Hourly sales made.
      - Monthly sales made.

        ### Results or Findings
        ---
        - Latte coffee generated the highest revenue.
        - Most sales occur in the afternoon.
        - Highest revenue comes in March.
        - Sales peak at the 10th hour of the day.
        - Most sales happen on Monday and Tuesdays.

          ### Recommendation
          ---
          - Invest in marketing and promotions.
          - Focus on expanding and promoting other products not doing well.
          - Introduce discount on sales to maximize profits.
